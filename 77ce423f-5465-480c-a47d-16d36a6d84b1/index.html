John Harding, California Family Law Lawyer

John E. Harding, JD, CFLS is a divorce lawyer and mediator. Certified as a specialist in the practice of family law by The State Bar of California Board of Legal Specialization, he has the distinction of being a Fellow in the American Academy of Matrimonial Lawyers (one of only fourteen in the San Francisco east bay) and the International Academy of Family Lawyers (one of only ten in all of Northern California). He has an AV rating by Martindale-Hubbell® Peer Review Ratings. He is listed in Best Lawyers and he has been a Northern California Super Lawyer from 2004 through 2015. He carries a perfect 10.0 rating from Avvo.
